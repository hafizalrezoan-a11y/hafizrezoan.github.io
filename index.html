I specialise in multidisciplinary geospatial research, technical reporting, and the kind of comprehensive documentation that helps complex environmental projects actually finish. Over the last seven years I've coordinated activities across agencies and stakeholders — from local field surveys in coastal Bangladesh to journal-level scientific writing.

My core competencies sit across Geographic Information Systems, remote sensing, UAV surveying, drone data processing, and photogrammetric analysis. That toolkit increasingly includes Python, R, and deep-learning workflows inside ArcGIS Pro.

I'm most useful where geospatial technology meets research method — translating raw imagery, sensor data, and field samples into evidence that supports real decisions.
